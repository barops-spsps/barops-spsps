About Us

The SPSPS Faculty of Law Bar Operations is a student-led initiative dedicated to supporting aspiring lawyers as they prepare for the Philippine Bar Examinations. Through mentorship, review programs, and community-driven funding, we stand behind every bar candidate on their journey to the profession.
